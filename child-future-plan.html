Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

સામાન્ય પ્રશ્નો

My child is 10 years old. Is it too late to start?

Not at all. LIC Jeevan Tarun accepts children up to age 12. Amritbaal accepts up to 13. Even starting now gives you 8–10 years of accumulation with LIC bonuses. Vinodbhai will calculate the corpus you can build by your child's 18th birthday.

What if I can't pay premiums for some months?

LIC offers a 30-day grace period. If a child plan lapses, it can usually be revived within 2–5 years. The premium waiver rider means even if you pass away, the policy continues — your child's future is protected.

How much will I get back at maturity?

This depends on the plan, sum assured, and bonus rate. LIC's bonuses have historically been consistent. Vinodbhai can show you a realistic illustration — he will never promise numbers higher than what LIC actually delivers.

Should I choose LIC child plan or a mutual fund SIP for my child?

LIC child plans give you guaranteed corpus + life cover + bonus + premium waiver in one product. MF SIPs give market-linked returns but no protection if you are not there. Many Vadodara parents do both — a child plan as the guaranteed floor and an SIP for extra upside.
