Lapsed LIC Policy? Don't Panic.
It Can Be Revived.

A lapsed policy is not a lost policy — not yet. LIC allows revival within 5 years of the first unpaid premium. Vinodbhai has helped dozens of Vadodara families revive their policies. Here's exactly what to do.

First — How Long Has It Lapsed?

Within grace period (30 days of due date): Pay the overdue premium immediately — policy is not lapsed yet, just in arrears.

1 month to 6 months: Simple revival — pay overdue premiums with interest. No medical examination typically required.

6 months to 3 years: Standard revival — overdue premiums + interest + possible revival form + medical depending on plan and sum assured.

3 years to 5 years: Special revival scheme (available periodically from LIC) — OR full revival with medical examination and higher requirements.

After 5 years: Policy cannot be revived. It may only be surrendered or paid-up. Do not wait — call Vinodbhai immediately if you are in this situation.

Step by Step

LIC Policy Revival — Step by Step

  • 1
    Locate your policy bond and premium receipt

    Find your original LIC policy document and any premium receipts you have. Note your policy number — you'll need this for every step.

  • 2
    Check revival eligibility

    Contact Vinodbhai with your policy number. He will check how long the policy has been lapsed, which revival scheme applies, and calculate the exact amount needed.

  • 3
    Gather required documents

    Depending on lapse duration and sum assured: Aadhaar/PAN, address proof, and possibly a health declaration or medical report. Vinodbhai tells you exactly what's needed.

  • 4
    Pay the revival amount to LIC

    Pay all overdue premiums with interest through LIC's online portal (licindia.in), LIC branch, or authorized collection centre. Vinodbhai can accompany you to the branch.

  • 5
    Submit revival form if required

    For longer lapses, LIC Form 680 (declaration of good health) or a medical report is required. Vinodbhai helps you fill and submit this accurately.

  • 6
    Receive revival confirmation from LIC

    LIC issues a revival receipt and updates the policy. Your policy is now active again — with all future benefits restored.

Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to revive a lapsed LIC policy?

You pay all overdue premiums plus LIC's interest rate on the unpaid premiums (typically 9–10% per annum). Vinodbhai will calculate the exact amount before you proceed — no surprises.

What if I can't afford to pay all overdue premiums at once?

Under some LIC special revival schemes, you may be allowed to pay a portion upfront. Contact Vinodbhai — he will advise on the best approach based on your specific policy and financial situation.

I lapsed my policy 4 years ago. Can it still be revived?

Yes — LIC allows revival within 5 years. You are still within the window, but time is running short. WhatsApp or call Vinodbhai immediately — he will tell you exactly what can be done.

What happens to my accumulated bonus if the policy lapsed?

Bonuses already declared (before lapse) remain attached to the policy and are not lost during revival. Bonuses for the lapse period are forfeited — but the existing bonus is preserved once the policy is revived.
