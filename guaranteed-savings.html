Frequently Asked Questions

સામાન્ય પ્રશ્નો

What is a 'reversionary bonus' in LIC plans?

Each year, LIC declares a 'Simple Reversionary Bonus' as a percentage of sum assured. This bonus is added to your policy's value every year and paid out at maturity or on death — whichever comes first. LIC's bonus rates have been stable for decades.

Can I take a loan against my LIC endowment policy?

Yes. After your policy acquires surrender value (typically after 3 years of premium payment), you can take a loan of up to 90% of surrender value directly from LIC at competitive interest rates — without breaking the policy.

What is the effective return on LIC endowment plans?

Effective returns on LIC endowment plans are typically in the 5–6.5% range (IRR) for policies held to maturity, depending on bonus rates and premium payment term. The key advantage is that this return is tax-free under 10(10D), with life cover bundled in.
